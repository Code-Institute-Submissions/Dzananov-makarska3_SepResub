About Makarska

Makarska is a part of Split and is located att the Dalmatia coast-line. It is locatdet between Bikovo Mountain and the Adriatic sea. Makarska has a beautiful center beach, trending cafes and tasty resturants. It has a beautiful view at the Bikovo Mounatins from the whole town. Makarska is a good starting point when visiting Brac, Hvar and Jelca.Nightlife in Makarska is fun both for familys with children and party people. On this site you will find some breef information about Makarska and what you can do when in Makarska.

When in Makarska, you have the opportunity to go sightseeing to Brac and Jelca by boat. You can book the one-day trip at the docks in Makarska. All boats go to both Jelca;where you can experience "The city of Lavendel". Its a cozy village with beautiful streets and aquablue water. You also get to visit the populare and famous beach Brac.
